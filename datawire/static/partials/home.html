Automatically keep track a range of data sources and be informed whenever an action — a government contract, company registration, or criminal arrest — mentions the name of one of the topics of your investigations.

datawi.re is powered through a decentralized network of data stringers, small tools that monitor data feeds and submit new records that'll be matched to the names of people, organizations, topics and places that you've set up to monitor.

What kind of data do we track?

  • Worldwide: Company creations and directorship changes
  • European Union: European Tenders and Contract Awards, State Aid Register, Corporate Mergers
  • Germany: Vorgänge in Bundestag und Bundesrat, BaFin Directors Dealings, Förderkatalog der Bundesregierung,
  • Great Britain: Central Government Expenditure
